02

For Lily — please confirm / mull over

① Denisova 3 — was the chromosome naming an accident?

Denisova 3's BAM is the only one using chr-prefixed contigs (chr1, chrM); every other sample and the reference use bare names (1, 2). So a ~30× Denisovan comes out near-empty at the pigmentation loci and lands in the sparse group of Fig 5. If that mismatch was unintentional, we re-run it with matched contigs and it should jump to the high-coverage tier — which would also move its position in the PCA and missingness results.

② The big one — the pigmentation panel is on hg38 but the data is hg19

This is now confirmed with evidence (it's why the pigmentation PCA came out flat, and it also shifts the depth measurements). Did you know the GWAS/panel coordinates were hg38 while the SGDP + archaic data are hg19? And how do you want to harmonize — lift the data up to hg38 (matches the plan and the four datasets) or lift the panel down to hg19? See B2.

③ One quick confirm

Was the whole-genome calling's --ploidy 1 (haploid) intentional? It means no heterozygous genotypes on autosomes. (Also: there are two ancient-calling scripts, -mv vs -c — the -c one is clearly the one used, so we'll just delete the other.)

Analysis redesign

from the 17 Jun plan

Data & coordinates

The unblocking step — B2 likely also fixes A2 and enables B1.

  • B1Four-dataset coverage analysis🔴TLLH
    Layered coverage: GWAS-only, then each of the four subsets. Source: pigmentation-gene-network.
  • B2Normalize everything to hg38🔴TLLH
    Datasets already hg38; lift the archaic + SGDP side from hs37d5/hg19. Likely resolves A2.

"What color were they?"

Simple, fast, defensible — get the landscape out first.

  • B3Directionality polygenic score + MC1R check🟡TLLH
    GWAS betas → +1/−1 per pigment-increasing/decreasing allele. Reconfirm no MC1R red-hair loss-of-function. HIrisPlex-S coefficients optional (European-trained → directionality is the safer route for archaics).

PCA, redone

Three variants × two designs.

  • B4Three-variant PCA🟡LH
    (i) pigmentation SNPs · (ii) whole pigmentation-related genes (pull start/stop coords) · (iii) genome-wide. Designs: everybody-together (moderns + 3 high-cov) & moderns-only with all ancients projected.
  • B5Missingness strategy🟡TLLH
    Masking all → <50 SNPs; focus high-coverage for SNP work, move to gene-level to escape sparsity.
  • B6Genes-vs-SNPs layers🟡YPTL
    Network/Baxter/Bajpai = genes (coords); GWAS/HIrisPlex = SNPs. Keep explicit.
  • B7Functional / skin-specific focus⚪YPLH
    Report syn:non-syn ratios; highlight skin-specific genes (TYR, OCA2, TYRP1, DCT, PMEL, MLANA).
